Career Guidance Services

For your Career World application, you can categorize the services into three main areas based on the core functionalities you provide. Here are three key services:

Aptitude Testing and Career Recommendations

This service is designed to help students understand their strengths, interests, and capabilities through an aptitude test. The system evaluates their answers and provides tailored recommendations on career options and educational paths based on their results.


Career Guidance and Expert Consultations

This service offers students access to expert advice and consultations to help them make informed decisions about their future. By providing direct access to professionals, the system bridges the gap between students and expert career counselors.



Educational Path Exploration

This service provides students with a comprehensive exploration of educational and career paths based on their academic background (e.g., after 10th, after 12th). It helps students navigate available courses and career opportunities by offering detailed information and comparisons of different career tracks.

Talk Guidance

Career Guidance Application allows students to connect with experts for career advice and insights. This feature, available through a "Take Guidance" button, enables users to initiate conversations with experts using their Google accounts. It's designed to offer students personalized, real-time career advice, helping them navigate their education and career paths by consulting professionals directly.
